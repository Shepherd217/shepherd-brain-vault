# HATCHLY — PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT
Version 1.0 | May 2026

## WHAT HATCHLY IS

A idea validation platform. You bring an idea. Hatchly tells you if it's worth pursuing — before you spend money, time, or energy building it.

**One input. One verdict. Real research behind it.**

## WHAT HATCHLY IS NOT

- Not a builder — we don't build your product
- Not a coding tool — not competing with Lovable, Bolt, Replit
- Not a trend tracker — not Exploding Topics or Google Trends
- Not a market research firm — not a 50 page PDF that confuses you
- Not a cheerleader — we will tell you to walk away if the data says so

## THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

People waste real money and real time building things nobody wants.

**The evidence:**
- 42% of startups fail because there's no market need
- Founders spend months building in isolation then launch to silence
- Vibe coding tools let people build fast but validate nothing
- Every existing validator either hedges its verdict or dumps data without direction
- Nobody starts with the human — every tool starts with "describe your app"

**The target:** 29.8 million solopreneurs in the US alone. Average income $39,273. Aspiration $219,000. The gap between those two numbers is largely explained by building the wrong things.

## WHO WE SERVE

**Primary:** Solopreneurs with an idea — could be a SaaS, service, content business, physical product, tool, MCP, SDK, anything — who need to know if it's worth pursuing before they commit resources.

**Their emotional state:**
- They've been burned before — spent money on credits, got nothing
- They're busy — family, job, life — can't afford to waste time
- They want honest signal not polite encouragement
- They need someone to tell them the truth

**They are not:**
- Technical founders who can validate themselves
- Funded startups with research budgets
- People who just want validation that confirms what they already believe

## THE PRODUCT FLOW

### Step 1 — DISCOVER
- Conversational. Emotional. No forms.
- "What breaks your heart when you see it go unsolved?"
- Surfaces the real idea. Sets honest expectations.
- "We're going to put this through real research. Are you ready for an honest answer?"

### Step 2 — VALIDATE
- Hermes-level research runs against the idea.
- Reddit pain signals. Competitor wounds. Search demand. Willingness to pay.
- Returns: **BUILD / TWEAK / WALK AWAY**
- With sources. Not vibes.

### Step 3 — LOCATE
- Finds exactly where the target customer lives online.
- Specific subreddits. Facebook groups. Newsletters. Communities.
- Not "post on social media." Specific and actionable.

### Step 4 — BRAND
- Name suggestions. Color direction. Tone of voice. One-liner positioning.
- DESIGN.md file generated.
- Looks like you hired a $50K agency.

### Step 5 — BRIEF
- One page. Takes to any builder, freelancer, or AI tool.
- "Build this." Done.

## WHAT MAKES THE VERDICT TRUSTWORTHY

This is the core product risk. A wrong verdict destroys trust permanently.

**The research layer is what makes it real:**
- Real Reddit thread analysis
- Real competitor pricing and complaint data
- Real search volume and trajectory
- Real community existence check
- Sources shown — not hidden

**TWEAK verdict** always includes 3 specific numbered changes. Not "adjust positioning." Specific.

## BUSINESS MODEL

### Free tier:
- Steps 1 and 2 only. Discovery conversation and verdict.
- No signup required.
- This is the trust builder and the top of funnel.
- If the verdict is accurate — they come back and pay.

### Paid tier — $29/month:
- Full flow. Steps 1-5.
- Discover, validate, locate, brand, brief.
- Unlimited validations.

**Why $29:**
- Cheaper than one wasted month on Lovable credits.

**Why subscription not one-time:**

- They come back. They bring friends.
- Recurring revenue compounds.
