# Hatchly

## Concept
Service where you have your business idea "hatched" — **not a builder, not a CMO**. This is market validation: "Is there a market, need, desire for your idea and is the market saturated?"

A whole new angle not on the streets yet. Counter to Lovable, Rork, etc. where you waste money building an idea that falls flat on launch.
