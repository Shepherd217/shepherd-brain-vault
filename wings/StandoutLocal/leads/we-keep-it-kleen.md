## Pain Points Found

1. **PRIMARY DOMAIN DOWN** — wekeepitkleen.com returns SSL error + "Site not published." Anyone typing the domain manually or clicking an old link gets a dead page. This is losing customers every day.

2. **Yahoo Email** — wekeepitkleen2012@yahoo.com screams "small operation that hasn't invested in their brand." Every customer sees this. Every prospect doubts professionalism.

3. **Template Placeholder Text** — Multiple pages contain unedited Hibu template text: "Bullet text", "Title or Question", "Describe the item or answer the question..." This is live on their public site.

4. **No Online Booking** — Phone-only scheduling. In 2026, competitors have "Book Now" buttons, calendar integration, instant quotes. Phone-only = friction.

5. **No Review Embedding** — 90+ five-star reviews exist but aren't displayed on the site. The reviews page just asks people to leave reviews. Social proof = invisible.

6. **H1 Typography Error** — "Professional CleaningCompany" — no space between "Cleaning" and "Company." First impression on every visitor.

7. **.NET vs .COM Confusion** — They have TWO domains. .com is dead. .net works. Customers don't know which to use. Brand fragmentation.
