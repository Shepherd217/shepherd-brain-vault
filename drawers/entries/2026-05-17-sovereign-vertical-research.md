## 1. Healthcare Data Refinery (#2)

### Market Validation
- **AI in Healthcare market:** $14.92B (2024) → **$110.61B by 2030**, CAGR **38.6%** (MarketsandMarkets, May 2025)
- **Growth driver:** "Rising demand for advanced health services that leverage AI, ML, and data analytics"
- **Key players:** Microsoft, NVIDIA, Philips, Siemens Healthineers, GE HealthCare, Epic Systems
- **Fastest-growing segment:** Cloud-based deployment (CAGR 41.7%)

### Scalable?
✅ **YES.** Data is infinitely replicable. Once you build scrapers for PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, and FDA datasets, you can:
- Sell to researchers, pharma, biotech, health tech startups
- Layer AI models on top (diagnosis, drug discovery, population health)
- Expand from datasets → analytics → insights → API access

**Scaling factor:** Content → API → Platform

### Distributable?
⚠️ **MODERATE.** Distribution challenges:
- **Regulatory friction:** HIPAA, FDA, EU AI Act compliance required
- **Customer acquisition:** Academic/research buyers are slow, bureaucratic
- **Trust threshold:** Medical data requires institutional credibility

**Distribution path:** Direct sales to research institutions → API marketplace (RapidAPI) → partnership with existing health data platforms

### Will It Work?
✅ **YES, but with caveats:**
- **Revenue model $10-30/dataset is realistic** for niche clinical datasets
- **Competition is ENTERPRISE-grade** (Microsoft, NVIDIA) — you compete on AGILITY, not scale
- **Moat:** Speed of dataset assembly + AI-ready formatting + niche focus (e.g., "rare disease trials only")
- **Time to first dollar:** 2-4 weeks (build scrapers → list on marketplace)
